Attire Guide

We would love to see everyone dressed in the spirit of our celebration. Here's a quick guide to help you choose your outfit for the day.

For the Ladies
Elegant & Modest
  • Floor-length dress preferred
  • Church-appropriate attire, or bring a cover-up for the ceremony
  • Please follow our color theme below
For the Gentlemen
Barong & Earth Tones
  • Barong Tagalog
  • Khaki or brown pants
  • Closed shoes appropriate for the wedding

Our color theme is in warm, earthy tones — think browns, rust, and soft neutrals. We kindly ask guests to avoid wearing white or any shade that may compete with the bridal party.
